# Skyworks-Neo

Welcome to **Skyworks-Neo**, an innovative organization originating from Tsinghua University's Skyworks initiative.

## About Us

Skyworks-Neo is a collaborative effort dedicated to advancing embedded systems, computer hardware, and aviation technologies. We are supported by **Edgewalker (CANTON) Co., Ltd**, a leading partner in our mission to drive technological excellence.

### Our Focus Areas

- 🖥️ **Embedded Systems** - Cutting-edge solutions for resource-constrained environments
- ⚙️ **Computer Hardware** - Optimized hardware architectures and design innovation
- ✈️ **Aviation** - Advanced technologies for the aerospace industry
- 🔧 **Server Operations & Network Technologies** - Infrastructure management and network optimization
- 🖨️ **3D Printing & Carbon Fiber** - Advanced manufacturing and composite material solutions
- 🔌 **Firmware Modification** - Custom firmware development and optimization
- 🛠️ **Computer Repair & Maintenance** - Professional hardware diagnostics and repair services
- 🚀 **GPU Auto-Performance Toolchain** - Our flagship product for GPU optimization and performance analysis

## What We Do

We specialize in developing the **GPU Auto-Performance Toolchain**, a comprehensive suite of tools designed to:
- Automatically analyze GPU performance metrics
- Optimize compute workloads
- Provide actionable insights for hardware acceleration
- Enable seamless integration across embedded and high-performance computing environments

Our comprehensive service portfolio spans from infrastructure management to advanced manufacturing, ensuring end-to-end technical excellence.

## Our Heritage
